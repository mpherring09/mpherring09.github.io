**Synopsis:**

Light-Speed Station is a powerful testing facility with a train that can move almost the speed of light. With help from their lab assistants, the player must complete several experiments to discover the nature of fast moving objects.​​​​​​​

**Project Components:**
- Simulation of relativistic effects (time dilation, length contraction, and relativity of simultaneity)
- Scripts for several experiments with explanatory dialogue
- Sequencing system to guide the player through these experiments in order
- UI design and assets for dialogue, menus, and in-game clocks
- Incorporation and manipulation of external models, textures, and sounds
- Custom models and animations
